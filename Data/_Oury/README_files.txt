The following files are online appendices for "From Genomics to Integrative Taxonomy? The Case Study of Pocillopora Corals" by Oury et al.
Appendix1_Sampling.docx
Appendix2_Molecular_analyses.docx
Appendix3_Morphological_analyses.docx
Appendix4_Symbiodiniaceae_analyses.docx
Appendix5_Illustrations.docx


DB_Pocillopora_genomic_364ind.xlsx contains the genetic data related to Pocillopora colonies (sampling, morphotypes, ORF and PocHistone haplotypes, microsatellite genotypes...)

Micromorphometry_Pocillopora_170ind.xlsx contains the morphometric data from the 170 Pocillopora colonies morphologically analysed

Pocillopora_UCEs_exons_2068_ref_sequences.fasta contains the 2,068 reference sequences de novo constructed

Pocillopora_361ADN_1559SNP.vcf = Final SNP dataset used randomly keeping one SNP per locus

Pocillopora_361ADN_17465SNP.vcf = Final SNP dataset used with all SNP

Symbiodiniaceae_552ASVx259samples_with_taxonomy.txt = Final ASV table for Symbiodiniaceae analyses
